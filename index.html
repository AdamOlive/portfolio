About Me

Hi! I'm Adam, a technical game designer with 5 years of experience designing and developing games and Metaverse experiences. Much of my career has been working on Crayta, a UGC game-creation platform for PC and mobile, where I led a major design overhaul on the Hub, developed a wide variety of games and experiences, helped design features such as touch input support and demonstrated to investors.

I've also developed UI and metagame systems on an unreleased Unity mobile game and released 3 personal Unity games, and won a BAFTA Young Game Designer's award in 2014. I'm currently taking a Certificate in Filmmaking course with the National Film and Television School to broaden my creative skillset.

I love bridging the gap between tech and design in the digital space, and I'm always striving to expand my skills in both areas. I'm passionate about accessible design, and believe simplifcation is a hugely important, often-overlooked aspect of the design process.
